What's it about?
The agreement is being signed between client and business where the business would not share the confidential information to anyone other than the client.



Important Terms-
1. Subcontractor’s employees are not eligible for, and may not participate in, any employee benefits extended to SDG employees, including without limitation vacation pay, holiday pay or paid sick leave.
2. The agreement can be terminated by both the parties by written agreement of both parties.
3. This agreement is governed only by a single party.
4. Violation or breach of this agreement will be paid by the party.

Features may be added to Goibibo.com-
1. Latest reviews of customers for Flights and Hotels will let customers choose from several options accordingly.

3. Seat availability(Flights) notification - Sometimes it happens that a particular rider cancels its filght. Airlines looks for a rider and an average joe is in need of a flight. By enabling this feature, if a customer is interested in a specific flight. That customer will get an instant push-notification about any avalaible seat in the flight. There will be a time limit beyond which we can not cancel or book our flights.
